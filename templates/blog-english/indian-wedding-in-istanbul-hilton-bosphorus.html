Indian Wedding in Istanbul-Rida and Adi /Hilton Bosphorus

Hello everyone!

Today I am gonna tell you a very special story. Rida and Adi are living in USA and their families are from India and Pakistan. They decided to do a destination wedding and fchoose a fairy tale city, Istanbul!

It was a 2 day long wedding. First day in the early morning they celebrate their marriage in a traditional way. I can’t share those moments because it was very private event..After lunch Rida and Adi prepared for the night. After first very romantic first look session we did our couple shooting. We were very lucky because the weather was sunny even it was the end of October.

During the couple shooting my team took the photos and videos of decor. Then the cocktail started, people socialize with each other and we documented those moments too..

You can understand the energy of Adi and his family from the photos of their entrance! They danced like no-one was watching them and everybody was shocked =) - we are too!-

Rida and her family came into ballroom a little bit romantic way. So they met and the tears were everywhere..

Half of the night was about talkings and special dance events which their friends and family perform for them. This part was so meaningful for everybody.

After speeches and dances the party started! And the night goes on..

Second day in the morning they did another traditional ceremony. ( this time I can share=)) Before ceremony again we did a couple shooting because their outfit was perfect also we had to keep their love and those romantic moments..

So this is the story of a two people who loved each other very much! Not only themselves but the families also very happy with this marriage..Also I think they are very lucky to have those friends who came to Turkey from all corners of the world..I hope you enjoy the photos and short video as much as I did..
