Monica-Yongyi / Istanbul Pre-Wedding Photos

Hello Everybody,

It has been a perfect year for me. I met lots of nice people while I am shooting them. I am planning to write blog posts as much as I can. So here is the first one about Monica and Yongyi.

Monica and Yongyi came to Istanbul for a vacation just before their wedding.. And they want to have a pre-wedding photo session with me. We planned to see iconic Istanbul views as a background. So I suggested them to start shooting very early in the morning. It was because I want the perfect light and pure Istanbul without any crowd.

We met at 6:00 am at Sultanahmet. It was very impressive to see Ayasofia at that time. There were nobody around just us and maybe few people.

From there we went to Galata Bridge. The sun was just rising and the light was fascinating. We use the bridge and sea as our decors.

When we reached to the Galata Tower it was still very early. So after the Tower shots we were able to shoot in peopleless Taksim streets.

In the end we experienced pure Istanbul with an awesome sunlight beside the photos we had.

So here you are my gorgeous couple Monica and Yongyi. I hope you like the photos as much as I love..
