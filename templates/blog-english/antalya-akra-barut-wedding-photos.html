Funda-Josep / Antalya Akra Barut Wedding Photos

Hello to everyone!

The first blog post of 2020 belongs to Funda and Josep, one of the most naive works of last year.

Funda and Josep live in Germany, but since Funda's family is in Antalya, they decided to have their wedding in the wonderful atmosphere of Akra Barut Hotel.

Every moment of the day, the sweet excitements, emotional moments, etc. were experienced in the best way and I was very lucky to kept those moments for the future. It was a wonderful Antalya wedding for me to experience and photograph. I hope you can feel that day with the photos I took..
