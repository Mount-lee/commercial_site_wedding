Ciragan Palace Wedding Photos-Seyma and Orazio

Hello everyone!

Today I am gonna tell you about a great couple Seyma and Orazio. It was a mixed culture wedding in a magnificent place: Cıragan Palace! Orazio is an Italian and Seyma is Turkish. They are both designers. I am sure you are gonna realize this detail from their choices =)

We started the day in Ciragan Palace with preparations. Seyma and her bridemaids had a lot of fun during this session. In other room Orazio also was getting ready with his dad.

When they are ready the ceremony started. Whole family and friends were there and we saw lots of tears and joy =)

After the ceremony we did family shooting and then started couple shooting. Cıragan Palace is one of the important building in Turkey! So I was very lucky. Because wherever I directed my camera I saw fabulous views. (the building, history and also Bosphorus)

Then the party started!

I hope you like the photos as much as I did. Also after the phots you can find the video about this gorgeous day!
